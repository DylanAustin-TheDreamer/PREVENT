PREVENT - 101

What is PREVENT?

PREVENT, is the act in which a fellow community has the duty to protect against the following aggrevates:

  • Neglect
  • Abuse
  • Radicalism
  • Extremism
  • Criminal Activity

PREVENT is a community effort...

It is the attempt to recognize and report concerns about an individual's behavior or circumstances that may indicate a risk of harm.
